Time to Book

How we came up with this idea?

Long Lines and Wait Times

We noticed that students often face difficulties when it comes to finding available dorm beds or dining hall seats. Long lines, overcrowded spaces, and poor management can make campus life more stressful than it needs to be.

Student Experience

As students, we have experienced these frustrations ourselves. That's what we thought.

Our Goal

Our goal is to save time, reduce stress, and help everyone enjoy a more organized and convenient campus life.

How did we solve this?

1

Easy Sign Up

Signing up is fast and hassle-free! Simply use your account and name to access the platform. No need for lengthy forms or additional details—just log in and you’re ready to book dormitory beds or dining slots in seconds. It’s that easy to get started!

2

Simple Booking

With our platform, finding and booking a room is as simple as it gets. Explore available options in real-time, select your preferred spot, and reserve it instantly. No need for complicated steps—just convenience and efficiency at your fingertips!

3
✕

Students will not stand in line!

✓

Students simply choose a time for dinner, then scan the QR-code that will be in front of the dining hall.
